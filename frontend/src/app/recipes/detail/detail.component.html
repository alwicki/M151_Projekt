This is the recipe of the legendary basler flour soup

Ingredients

5 tbspwhite flour
50 gbutter
1onion, finely chopped
1 dlwhite wine
1 litremeat bouillon
¼ tspnutmeg
salt and pepper to taste

Cooking steps

1. Toast the flour in a pan over a medium heat, stirring constantly until it turns hazelnut brown, remove, allow to cool slightly.
2. Reduce the heat, warm the butter in the same pan. 3. Sauté the onion for approx. 3 mins.
4. Pour in the wine and reduce completely.
5. Return the flour to the pan, pour in the stock, bring to the boil while stirring with a whisk.
6. Reduce the heat, simmer for approx. 1 hr. while stirring occasionally, season.
